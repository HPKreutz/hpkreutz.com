Training Principles
💧
Hydration
Drink at least half your body weight in ounces of water daily. Add 8oz per 20 minutes of intense activity.
😴
Recovery
Muscles grow during rest, not during the workout. Aim for 7–9 hours of sleep and honor your rest days.
🥩
Nutrition
Target 0.7–1g of protein per pound of bodyweight daily. Prioritize protein at each meal.
📈
Progressive Overload
Add reps, sets, or weight each week. Small consistent increases add up to massive results over time.
🔥
Warm Up
5–10 minutes before every session. Arm circles, leg swings, hip rotations, and light squats get the body ready.
✅
Consistency
Showing up every week matters more than any single perfect workout. Build the habit first.
Apple Cider Vinegar
🍎
Always Use Raw & Unfiltered
Look for "with the mother" on the label — Bragg's is the most popular brand.
💧
Always Dilute It
Never drink ACV straight — always mix with water or another liquid first.
⏰
Best Time to Take It
First thing in the morning on an empty stomach, or 20–30 minutes before a meal.
Tonic
Morning Wake-Up Tonic
Energy, digestion, blood sugar balance
Ingredients
1–2 tbsp ACV · 8oz warm water · 1 tbsp honey · ½ lemon juiced · pinch cayenne
How to Make
Mix all in a glass, stir until honey dissolves. Drink first thing in the morning.
💡 Cayenne boosts metabolism and lemon adds vitamin C — a powerful combo.
Tonic
Gut Health Tonic
Bloating, digestion, gut microbiome
Ingredients
1 tbsp ACV · 8oz water · 1 tsp honey · ¼ tsp ginger · ¼ tsp cinnamon
How to Make
Combine all and stir well. Drink 20 minutes before a meal.
💡 Ginger and cinnamon have anti-inflammatory properties that complement ACV.
