General Fitness — Bodyweight & Dumbbells
Training Principles
💧 Hydration
Drink at least half your body weight in ounces of water daily. Add 8oz per 20 minutes of intense activity.
😴 Recovery
Muscles grow during rest, not during the workout. Aim for 7–9 hours of sleep and honor your rest days.
🥩 Nutrition
Target 0.7–1g of protein per pound of bodyweight daily. Prioritize protein at each meal, don't skip breakfast.
📈 Progressive Overload
Add reps, sets, or weight each week. Small consistent increases add up to massive results over time.
🔥 Warm Up
5–10 minutes before every session. Arm circles, leg swings, hip rotations, and light squats get the body ready.
✅ Consistency Beats Intensity
Showing up consistently every week matters more than any single perfect workout. Build the habit first.
Apple Cider Vinegar
🍎
Always Use Raw & Unfiltered
Look for "with the mother" on the label — Bragg's is the most popular brand. The mother contains beneficial enzymes and probiotics.
💧
Always Dilute It
Never drink ACV straight — it can damage tooth enamel and irritate the esophagus. Always mix with water or another liquid first.
⏰
Best Time to Take It
First thing in the morning on an empty stomach, or 20–30 minutes before a meal. Rinse your mouth with water after drinking.
Tonic
Morning Wake-Up Tonic
Good for: Energy, digestion, blood sugar balance
Ingredients
· 1–2 tbsp apple cider vinegar
· 8 oz warm water
· 1 tbsp raw honey
· ½ lemon, juiced
· Pinch of cayenne pepper
How to Make It
Mix all ingredients in a glass and stir until honey dissolves. Drink first thing in the morning before eating.
💡 The cayenne boosts metabolism and the lemon adds vitamin C — a powerful morning combo.
Tonic
Gut Health Tonic
Good for: Bloating, digestion, gut microbiome
Ingredients
· 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
· 8 oz water (room temperature)
· 1 tsp raw honey
· ¼ tsp ground ginger
· ¼ tsp cinnamon
How to Make It
Combine all ingredients and stir well. Drink 20 minutes before a meal to support digestion and reduce bloating.
💡 Ginger and cinnamon both have anti-inflammatory properties that complement ACV's digestive benefits.
Tonic
Immune Booster Tonic
Good for: Immunity, inflammation, antioxidants
Ingredients
· 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
· 8 oz warm water
· 1 tbsp raw honey
· ½ tsp turmeric
· Pinch of black pepper
· ½ lemon, juiced
How to Make It
Mix all ingredients together. The black pepper activates the turmeric — don't skip it. Best taken daily during cold and flu season.
💡 Black pepper increases turmeric absorption by up to 2000% — a small pinch makes a big difference.

Fat Burner Shot
Good for: Weight management, appetite suppression, metabolism
Ingredients
· 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
· 2 oz water
· ½ tsp raw honey
· Pinch of cayenne pepper
· ¼ lemon, juiced
How to Make It
Mix in a shot glass and drink in one go. Take 30 minutes before a meal. Chase with a full glass of water.
💡 Studies suggest ACV can help reduce appetite and lower blood sugar spikes after meals.

Pre-Workout Energy Shot
Good for: Energy, endurance, electrolyte balance
Ingredients
· 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
· 2 oz coconut water
· ½ tsp raw honey
· Pinch of sea salt
· ¼ tsp ginger powder
How to Make It
Mix all ingredients in a shot glass. Take 15–20 minutes before your workout. The coconut water provides natural electrolytes.
💡 Sea salt replenishes sodium lost through sweat and helps with muscle function during exercise.
Detox Shot
Green Detox Shot
Good for: Detox, liver support, alkalizing the body
Ingredients
· 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
· 2 oz cucumber juice or water
· ½ tsp spirulina or wheatgrass
· ½ lemon, juiced
· 1 tsp raw honey
How to Make It
Blend or shake all ingredients together. Drink immediately in the morning on an empty stomach for best detox results.
💡 Spirulina is one of the most nutrient-dense foods on earth — this shot packs a serious nutritional punch.
Liver Detox
Classic Liver Flush Shot
Good for: Liver cleansing, bile production, toxin elimination
Ingredients
· 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
· 1 tbsp fresh lemon juice
· 1 tbsp olive oil (extra virgin)
· 1 clove garlic, minced
· 2 oz warm water
How to Make It
Blend or whisk all ingredients and take as a shot first thing in the morning. The olive oil stimulates bile flow which helps flush the liver.
💡 Garlic contains allicin and selenium — two compounds clinically shown to help protect and cleanse the liver.

Milk Thistle Power Shot
Good for: Liver cell repair, toxin filtering, hangover recovery
Ingredients
· 1 tbsp apple cider vinegar
· 1 tsp milk thistle powder
· 1 tbsp lemon juice
· 1 tsp raw honey
· 2 oz warm water

💡 Silymarin in milk thistle is one of the most studied liver-protective compounds — it actively repairs damaged liver cells.
